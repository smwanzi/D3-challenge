Health Risks vs Demographics

Have you ever wanted to know the relationship between certain health related factors and demographics? Wonder no more! Using data from the U.S. Census Bureau and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System, a few of those factors have been analysed and visualized below using Data-Driven Document (D3) from Javascript. The idea is to have fun exploring the relationship between health risk factors; Healthcare, Obesity, Smoking vs demograhics factors; Income, Poverty and Age. Click on any of the axis titles to start exploring!
